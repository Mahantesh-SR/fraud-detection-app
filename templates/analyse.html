Card Type VS Fraud

Card type dosen't contribute as much as other attributes does. Since it hovers between fraudulent and legitimate.

Frequency VS Fraud

Whenever hacker performs fraud he gives multiple attempts. As the number of transaction increases, chances of transaction being fraudulent also increases.

Merchant VS Fraud

Merchant type is almost independent, hackers can attack any person's account. Since it hovers between fraudulent and legitimate

Amount VS Fraud

Whenever the transaction amount is more than normal transaction, possibility of transaction being fraudulent will be more.
